# Cybersecurity Risk Assessment and Risk Register

## Overview

This project demonstrates a cybersecurity risk assessment conducted for a commercial banking environment. The assessment evaluated threats affecting critical assets, measured risk likelihood and business impact, and utilized a risk register to prioritize remediation efforts.

The objective was to identify, analyze, and manage cybersecurity risks to support informed decision-making and improve organizational security posture.

## Project Objectives

The project focused on:

- Cybersecurity risk assessment
- Risk identification
- Risk scoring and prioritization
- Risk register development
- Business impact evaluation
- Threat analysis
- Remediation planning
- Security governance

---

## Risk Assessment Methodology

### Asset Assessed

**Bank Funds**

### Evaluation Method

Risk Priority = Likelihood × Severity

A qualitative risk matrix was used to determine which risks required immediate remediation and management attention.

## Key Findings

The assessment identified the following priorities:

### Critical Risk

**Financial Records Leak (Risk Score: 9)**

This risk requires immediate remediation due to the combination of high likelihood and severe business impact.

### High-Priority Risks

- Compromised User Database (Risk Score: 6)
- Business Email Compromise (Risk Score: 4)

Recommended mitigation measures include:

- Stronger encryption controls
- Enhanced access management
- Employee security awareness training
- Improved data protection mechanisms
